Assignments for March 6

Story Memo Idea: Send me some areas or topics or even questions you might like to answer using data. Hint: pick a topic you like or are already at least somewhat interested in. Aim for at least 2 ideas.

Data Cleaning: Using this official travel report, take the records of the Committee on Foreign Affairs trips between Oct. 31 and Dec. 31, 2013, and copy them into TextWrangler (if you don't have it on your laptop, it's a free download for the Mac). Then, using Find and Replace, put the records into tab-separated columns, remembering that you should replace multiple spaces with tabs, and also remove the extra . characters. As a finishing touch, fill in the empty name column where a person has multiple travel records. You should try this using the Fill in the Empty Cells technique we worked through in class.

SQL: Continuing with the senate voting database, write SQL to answer the following questions. You will need to study the fields in your tables.

  1. How many votes were decided by less than 10 votes?
  2. Show each vote result and how many times it occurred.
  3. Which senator voted "No" the most times on votes where the question was on the nomination?
  4. Which female senator missed the least votes?

Email me your story memo ideas, the excel file and the SQL from your queries by 5:20 p.m. on March 6.
