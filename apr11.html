Assignments for April 18

IRS Data: Download the DC list of non-profit organizations and, referring to the record layout and codes, determine how many organizations have a foundation code indicating that they receive "a substantial part of its support from a governmental unit or the general public", and how many of those have assets of at least $500,000. Which organization among those has the most assets? You can use SQLite or Excel to do this, and send me the answers and file.
