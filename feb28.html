Assignments for March 8

Mapping: Import into Excel the Fairfax Arrests file, save it as a CSV and then upload it to Fusion Tables. Make sure that the Address field is a "Location" field (use the Edit -> Change Columns drop down menu to check) and then geocode the file using that Address field. Visualize the results on a map and then click the "Share" button at the top right and enter my email (dw677@georgetown.edu) in the Add People box.

Email me the link to your Fusion Tables map by March 8.
