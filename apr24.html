Assignments for May 8

Story Memo: Email me (or post to your Github repository) your story memo, your data, plus an assessment of the source of your data that describes how easy or difficult it was to obtain, whether there was adequate documentation or explanations of it, and whether it was relatively easy to understand. In other words, grade the source of the data. If your files are too big or you'd rather send a link, that's fine. Please come to class prepared to discuss your story memos, too.
