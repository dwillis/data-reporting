Assignments for March 21

Open Refine: Download and install OpenRefine. Take the Fairfax Arrests file and open it in Excel and save it as a CSV file. Import it into Open Refine and then use a Text Facet on the Age column to find the THREE most frequent ages in the data.

Mapping: Export the Fairfax Arrests file as a CSV from Open Refine and upload it to Fusion Tables (be sure to switch to "Classic Look" under the Help menu). Make sure that the Address field is a "Location" field (use the Edit -> Modify Columns drop down menu to check) and then geocode the file using that Address field. Visualize the results on a map and then click the "Share" button at the top right and enter my email (dwillis@gmail.com) in the Add People box.

Reading: Look at this site of Military Personnel Statistics and, using any of the tables available there (including PDFs), come up with 2 questions that could be posed to the data. By questions, I mean something more sophisticated than "How many people served in the Navy in 2011?" Instead, try to come up with questions that require comparison or historical context.

Email me the age column answer from the Open Refine file, the link to your Fusion Tables map and your questions by 5:20 on March 21.
