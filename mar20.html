Assignments for March 27

Mapping: Using this CSV of motor vehicle thefts in DC in 2013, follow steps 3-7 of the Tribune's mapping exercise we did in class. Remember to go step-by-step and not skip anything. Then, after you map the incidents, try to summarize them in a paragraph or two using both the map and the original data (you can use Excel or SQLite to do any analysis, pointing out where in the city most seem to occur and where they are less common. You're the reporter: tell me what's in this data.

Story Memo: Update your log of your activities. Here's an example of a finished one from a previous class.

Readings: USA Today on demographic changes in the past 20 years, Noah Veltman on How To Not Screw Up Your Data.

Share your finished Fusion Table with me (dw677@georgetown.edu) and email your summary description, along with your story memo log, by 5:20 on March 27.
